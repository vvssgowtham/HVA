Badmintion

Badminton is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net. It is a popular recreational activity and competitive sport, with various forms of play, including singles and doubles. History Badminton originated in the 19th century at Badminton House in Gloucestershire, England. The modern version of the game has its roots in ancient Greece, China, and India, where similar games were played.

A podcast is a series of digital audio files released on a regular schedule, typically featuring one or more hosts discussing a particular topic or theme. Podcasts combine the intimacy of radio with the flexibility of online content, allowing creators to produce shows on a wide range of subjects, from science and culture to fiction and true crime.
