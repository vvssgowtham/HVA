MacBook

Apple’s MacBook line is a series of Mac notebook computers designed and marketed by Apple. The MacBook brand replaced the PowerBook and iBook brands during the Mac transition to Intel processors in 2005.

MacBook Air: A lightweight, ultra-portable laptop with a 13.6" or 15.3" Liquid Retina display, up to 18 hours of battery life, and Apple’s M2 or M3 chip. MacBook Pro: A powerful laptop with a 14.2" or 16.2" Liquid Retina XDR display, up to 22 hours of battery life, and Apple’s M3, M3 Pro, or M3 Max chip.
